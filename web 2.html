Our History

it was founded by 5 ordinary students who shared a passion for craft beverages. they decided to start an alcohol company to fill a gap in the market for innovative high quality drinks. with their diverse skills they created “JECJA”. they transformed a small space into a micro distillery and perfected a unique blend of traditional and modern flavors. their hard work and dedication turned JECJA into a renowned brand proving that ordinary students can achieve extraordinary success through collaboration and determination.
